About - Sanvidhan IAS CLasses

Sanvidhan IAS Classes is the institution established with the sole aim to initiate, enable and empower individuals to grow up to be extraordinary Civil Servants. The Academy persistently works towards realizing the dreams of both the Hindi and English medium students to participate in the UPSC, CGPSC, CGVYAPAM, BANK RAILWAY & other Civil Services Examination. Apart from conveying knowledge of the concern subjects, the Sanvidhan IAS Classes believes in the transformation of their students into a developed personality through leadership development, social responsibility and brain power development in order to ensure success in brilliantly modelling their respective career paths and to grow up to be extra-ordinary citizens of the country.

Why Choose Us?

Sanvidhan IAS Classes aims to develop amongst its students a competitive attitude along with sound academic base with quality teaching and individual attention as its hallmark. The Academy organizes seminars and workshops with the help of civil servants and experts in soft skills to train the aspirants to think, feel and express like administrators. This comprehensive approach towards IAS preparation has received an overwhelming response and has led to the pan India presence of Sanvidhan Educational Academy.
